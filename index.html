Why Homeopathy

We take care of your healthy life

Homeopathy is a system of alternative medicine that was developed in the late 18th century by Samuel Hahnemann, a German physician. It is based on the principle of "like cures like," which means that a substance that causes symptoms in a healthy person can be used to treat similar symptoms in a sick person. In homeopathy, highly diluted substances derived from plants, minerals, or animals are used as remedies. These substances are prepared through a process called potentization, which involves serial dilution and vigorous shaking, known as succussion. The belief is that this process enhances the healing properties of the substance while minimizing any toxic effects. One of the fundamental principles of homeopathy is individualization.Homeopathic remedies are chosen based on the unique symptoms and characteristics of each person, rather than just targeting the disease itself. Homeopaths believe that the body has the ability to heal itself, and the remedies stimulate this innate healing response. Critics of homeopathy argue that the dilutions used in the preparation of remedies often result in solutions that contain little to no active ingredients. They contend that any perceived benefits from homeopathic treatments are due to a placebo effect or natural healing over time.
